🚗 How to Reach

  • By Bus: Manali is well connected by bus from major cities like Delhi and Chandigarh. From the bus stop, you can hire local transport to reach Vashisht Village.
  • By Train: The nearest railway station is Joginder Nagar Railway Station, located about 165 km away. Chandigarh Railway Station (300 km) offers better connectivity.
  • By Air: The nearest airport is Bhuntar Airport (Kullu-Manali Airport), which is about 50 km from Manali. You can hire a taxi to reach the temple.
  • By Road: Vashisht Temple is located just 3 km from Manali town. You can take a taxi, auto-rickshaw, or even walk to the temple from the main town.

Local Experiences

Jogini Waterfalls

A mesmerizing cascade nestled in the Himalayan mountains, Jogini Falls offers a spiritual and natural retreat. The 20-minute trek through picturesque valleys leads to this magnificent waterfall, considered sacred by locals. Perfect for photography and meditation, the falls create a peaceful sanctuary surrounded by pristine nature.

Hot Water Springs

Vashisht's natural hot water springs are renowned for their therapeutic properties. These ancient springs, housed in traditional stone temples, offer a unique blend of spiritual significance and natural healing. The mineral-rich waters are believed to cure various skin ailments and provide deep relaxation.

Hadimba Temple

This ancient cave temple, dedicated to Goddess Hadimba, is a remarkable example of traditional hill architecture. Built in 1553, its pagoda-shaped roof and intricate wood carvings showcase local craftsmanship. Surrounded by cedar forests, this sacred site offers a glimpse into the region's rich cultural heritage.
